Hackathon Event on AWS Cost Optimization

Posted by Saravanesh on January 15, 2025

Finally, I did it—I attended a hackathon! It had been nearly 8 years since I first urged my friends to join me in one, but somehow it never happened. Since then, I’ve always wanted to push myself into the unknown and see what I could achieve in such an event.

The hackathon was organized by BBI, forming random teams of five with a strict 5-hour time limit. We had a few days to connect with our team and build a rapport before the event. The topic was revealed just an hour before the timer began: AWS cost optimization and solving the challenge of idle resources.

Fortunately, the topic was right in my comfort zone. We quickly formulated a plan to build a solution that could:

  • Identify Idle Resources
  • Allow Users to Approve Resource Removal or Archiving
  • Automatically Execute Deletion or Archiving Actions
  • Visualize Cost Reductions Dynamically on a Dashboard

Each team member took on specific roles to get the project up and running:

  • One teammate (tagging them in this LinkedIn post!) worked on the frontend, leveraging Vercel to rapidly deploy a functional UI.
  • Another handled the backend database, ensuring a seamless connection between the frontend and data processing layers.
  • Both ensured that SSO integration kept the application secure and restricted to company personnel.
  • Another teammate focused on building a dashboard that dynamically displayed cost savings and other essential metrics.

Meanwhile, I rolled up my sleeves and dived into writing Lambda functions using Python’s Boto3 library. These functions:

  • Detected idle resources and calculated their associated costs.
  • Loaded the data into the database for user review.
  • Captured user-approved resource removals or archival actions and executed the necessary operations.

The dashboard was dynamic, updating instantly as approvals came through, providing real-time insights into cost savings.

A Test of Creativity and Teamwork

This hackathon was more than just a coding marathon—it tested our creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork. With only 5 hours, we had to think quickly and adapt. Despite the tight constraints, our solution stood out because it was both functional and adaptable across different environments.

There are many ways to approach cloud cost optimization, and AWS offers robust services for these tasks. However, our solution was designed for rapid deployment, making it ideal for quick implementations, even in high-pressure scenarios like hackathons.

Our effort paid off—we won! Beyond the victory, this project demonstrated the power of smart cloud strategies to maximize efficiency and business value. It was a reaffirmation of the magic of teamwork and innovation.

Project Highlights

  • Purpose: Identify inefficiencies in cloud spending and provide actionable insights for cost reduction.
  • Approach: Leverage AWS services like AWS Cost Explorer, S3, and Lambda to analyze and optimize cloud usage.
  • Outcome: Achieved a 20% cost reduction for the presented use case, visualized through dynamic dashboards showing real-time progress and insights.
